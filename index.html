Getting support for the older person you are helping

Answer a few questions about their situation—for example whether they wish to stay at home or move to an alternative living arrangement, so we can suggest options to explore.

We can then guide you through the kinds of decisions you may need to make, and the government support that can help you.

If you are helping a couple, please complete this set of questions twice.

3 to 5 minutes to complete
