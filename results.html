  • Help getting out and about4 items

    Normal ageing, medical conditions and medication may impact on a person’s ability to drive. There are alternatives for getting around, such as transport concession cards and taxi vouchers.

    Transport options

    There are many different transport options to help ageing people maintain their independence and ability to get around.
    Australian Human Rights Commission: Your rights in retirement to transport

    Driving ability

    When renewing your licence you may need to have a medical examination with your GP to confirm your fitness to hold a driver’s licence.
    SA: Medical fitness to drive

    Travel to and from appointments

    If an older person has a one-off appointment they may qualify to get taxi vouchers through their GP. For regular appointments they may qualify for transport assistance after being assessed through My Aged Care.
    My Aged Care

    Transport and taxi concessions

    South Australians who hold an approved concession card, receive a Centrelink payment or hold a Seniors Card can receive a concession on public transport fares. Asylum seekers may also be eligible for public transport concession fares. Find out how to apply for a concession. SA: Public transport concessions

  • Consider consent needs5 items

    There are many ways of documenting an aged person’s wishes and planning for any eventuality.

    Make a power of attorney

    There are two kinds of power of attorney. A general power of attorney appoints someone to manage your legal and financial affairs in the short term. An enduring power of attorney prepares for the eventuality of no longer being able to make decisions. They differ slightly from state to state.
    Powers of attorney in South Australia
    Powers of attorney by state

    Make an advanced care directive

    Captures a person’s wishes, values and treatments about their future medical care.
    Advance care planning in SA

    Prepare a will

    A will documents who receives a person’s assets when they die.
    Prepare a legal will

    Find out about organ donation

    You can nominate which organs and tissues to donate for transplantation. Decide what you would like to do and register any decision online.
    Organ and Tissue Authority
