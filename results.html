    Older people can be assessed for saftey and support at home for redisential care needs through My Aged Care a national governemt scheme.

    As a first step call My Aged Care to create a profile. They will ask you a series of quesins to work out if you need an assessment in your home, whether you need to be assessed for lower or highter needs, and what government subsidy is available for you.
