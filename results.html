  • Explore home help2 items

    Older people can be assessed for safety and support at home or for residential care needs through My Aged Care–a national government scheme. As a first step call My Aged Care to create a profile. They will ask you a series of questions to work out if you need an assessment in your home, whether you need to be assessed for lower or higher needs, and what government subsidy is available for you.
