As you selected a wish to stay at home (with modifications and/or support), here are steps you might like to consider and information on how to get the types of support you need.

Getting assessed for support

Older people can be assessed for safety and support at home through My Aged Care–a national government scheme.

As a first step call My Aged Care to register. They will ask a series of questions to work out the level of help needed and what government subsidies they are eligible for.

Modifications in the home

Looking into any safety and security needs is a good way to help an older person stay at home.

When you call My Aged Care they will arrange an assessment of the modifications you need, or give you a referral code to organise your own. The service provider who does your assessment will connect you to someone who will do the work.
