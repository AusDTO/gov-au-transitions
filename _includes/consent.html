There are many ways of documenting an aged person’s wishes and planning for any eventuality.

Make a power of attorney

There are two kinds of power of attorney. A general power of attorney appoints someone to manage your legal and financial affairs in the short term. An enduring power of attorney prepares for the eventuality of no longer being able to make decisions. They differ slightly from state to state.
